Privacy Policy

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

• Name
• Contact Information
• Email Address

We collect your personal information in order to:

• contact you regarding your online enquiry
• send your quote through to you via email

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter their contact information, we'll be unable to contact you regarding your enquiry.

• If you choose not to enter your email address, we'll be unable to send through your quote

We keep your information safe by storing it in our encrypted database and only allowing specific staff access to this information.

We keep your information for the life of your PC/service agreement at which point we securely destroy it by securely erasing all your data from our database.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at sales@zephyr.com, or (03) 569 9871, or Zephyr PC 167 Madras Street Christchurch, 8011 New Zealand.
